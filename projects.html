Projects

Here is a list of the projects I am involved or I worked on. The lis below includes both personal projects and major university projects. Notice that some of them cannot be reproduced/the source code is not shared due to Non Discloure Agreements about the files or sensitive data.

  • Personal Portfolio

May 2022

Indeed, the website you are visiting right now. It consists of few pages to describe experience, interests and projects, and it can work as a Resume extension.
Keywords HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

  • Cancer Fatigue Prediction

March 2021 - June 2021

Project carried out at the University of Twente, in collaboration with the Ruhr University Bochum. Its scope is to analyze a dataset containing patients (physical and mental) data and predict Cancer Related Fatigue, on a given scale.
Keywords R, Machine Learning, Supervised learning, Random Forest, Healthcare.

  • Library Management System (Client) (Server)

February 2021 - June 2021

A Java application that allows library workers to manage users, books and loans, based on Microservices and contenarized in Docker. It is composed of two parts, the Shell, i.e. the interface for the users, and the servers, i.e. the backend that listens for requests.
Keywords Java, Spring, Microservices, Docker, Kubernetes, APIs.

  • Blahut-Arimoto Implementation

February 2021 - March 2021

Java implementation of the Blahut-Arimoto algotithm, for the course of Information Theory and Statistics. It allows to compute the capacity of a channel given the probability matrix, to better understand the quality of such communication method.
Keywords Java, Channel capacity.

  • Wikipedia Dumps Analysis

November 2020 - January 2021

Analysis of hundreds of gigabytes of Wikipedia historical data to identify patterns in the edit history. It was performed on a computer cluster based on HDFS and using PySpark.
Keywords Python, Big Data, Spark, Hadoop, HDFS.

  • Healthcare web platform

April 2019 - January 2020

Web application involving Medics and their patients. Medics can prescribe drugs and suggestions to their patients, check their exams and view other related data. On the other side, patients can download doctors messages, book appointments and look for nearby pharmacies.
Keywords HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Servlet.
